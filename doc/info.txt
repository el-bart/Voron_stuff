# info

## board
* BTT Octopus 1.1
* based on `STM32F446`
* 4 heaters: HE0..HE3
  * 24V supply
  * (reportedly) 10A per steppers, 10A for bed, 10A for the rest
* hotend heating is reportedly u

## sensors
* chamber thermistor: semitec 103NT-4
* accelerometer (external): ADXL345 (over SPI)

## power
* main fuse: 8A, slow blow (1.2mm panel)
